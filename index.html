Hockey Training

To master triangle passing, you must first learn the lingo.

1. Flat pass: Any pass made directly to your right or left.
2. Through pass: Any straight forward pass.
3. Back pass: Any backwards pass.
4. Diagonal pass: Any pass made at an angle. However, diagonal passing is generally not used in triangle passing because these passes are easily intercepted.
